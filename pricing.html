Common questions

What happens after the free trial?

Your training data and history stay safe. You just won't get new AI-generated plans until you subscribe or purchase a goal. No pressure, no dark patterns.

Can I switch between monthly and yearly?

Yes. You can switch anytime from within the app. If you switch from monthly to yearly, the change takes effect at your next billing date.

Do goal purchases include everything Pro has?

Goal purchases include full AI coaching features for the duration of that goal. They're ideal if you have a specific race or target in mind rather than year-round coaching.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel from your app store subscription settings. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
