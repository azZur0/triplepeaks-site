Features

Everything you need to train smarter for triathlon, running, cycling, and swimming.

AI-Powered Weekly Plans

Every week, your AI coach builds a fresh training plan. It considers your fitness, fatigue, upcoming races, and how last week went. Multi-sport periodization means your swim, bike, and run blocks work together — not against each other.

  • Periodized training blocks: base, build, peak, taper
  • Multi-sport load balancing across swim, bike, and run
  • Progressive overload with weekly volume management

Workout Analysis

After every workout, your AI coach reviews pacing, heart rate response, and zone distribution. You get real analysis with execution scoring — not just "good job."

  • Pacing consistency and plan comparison
  • Heart rate zone distribution breakdown
  • Effort-adjusted performance scoring

Smart Adaptation

Life doesn't follow a training plan. When you miss workouts, get sick, or overperform, your plan adapts. Load gets redistributed across all sports so you stay on track without overtraining.

  • Multi-sport load balancing when sessions are missed
  • Illness and injury awareness with recovery protocols
  • Schedule flexibility without losing direction

AI Coach Chat

Ask your AI coach anything about your training. It knows your plan, your history, and your current fitness. Get answers that are specific to you — not generic advice.

  • "What's my Ironman pace target based on recent training?"
  • "Should I swim or run today? I'm feeling tired."
  • "How is my Olympic tri prep tracking?"

Strava Integration

Connect once, train forever. Your runs, rides, swims, and other activities sync automatically — including GPS, heart rate, pace, and power data.

  • Automatic multi-sport activity import via webhooks
  • Training history import for better initial plans
  • Works with any device that syncs to Strava

Race Planning

Set a goal race — from a Sprint triathlon to a full Ironman, from a 5K to an ultra marathon — and get a fully periodized plan working backwards from race day.

  • Goal-based periodization: Sprint, Olympic, 70.3, Ironman
  • Automated taper and race-week preparation
  • Post-race recovery planning
